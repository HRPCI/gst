Refund Policy – eFiling Portal Solutions

eFiling Portal Solutions provides consultancy assistance services for GST registration and related documentation support. The service fee charged on this website (₹990) is a professional consultancy fee for assistance in GST application preparation and submission.

Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund only if:

  • The request is made within 24 hours of payment, and
  • The document verification or application preparation process has not yet started

Non-Refundable Conditions

Once any of the following steps have started, the service fee becomes non-refundable:

  • Document verification
  • Application drafting
  • GST application submission
  • Consultation completed

Government approval timelines are controlled by the GST Department and are not under our control.

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.
