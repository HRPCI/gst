Eligibility

Who Needs GST Registration?

Registration is generally required if any of these apply to your business:

📦 Goods suppliers above ₹40 Lakhs turnover
🛠️ Service providers above ₹20 Lakhs turnover
🌐 Interstate sellers
🛒 E-commerce sellers
Checklist

Documents You'll Typically Need

Keep these ready before starting your application.

Individual / Proprietor

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Bank account details

Business Address Proof

  • Electricity bill
  • Rent agreement
  • No-objection certificate (NOC)

Company / LLP

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • PAN of the entity
  • Directors' / Partners' KYC
