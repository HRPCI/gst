Who Needs GST Registration?

GST registration is mandatory if your business turnover exceeds:

  • ₹40 Lakhs for goods suppliers (₹20 Lakhs in some states)
  • ₹20 Lakhs for service providers
  • Interstate sellers & E-commerce sellers must register regardless of turnover
